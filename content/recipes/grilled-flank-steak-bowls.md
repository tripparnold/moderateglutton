I love beef, I love switching up the cut of beef, and I love the sauce. Amber and I eat this weekly. It cooks quickly and doesn't require too many dishes if you grill the beef and microwave the green beans.

### **Ingredients**

**For the steak:**
- 1 lb flank steak (grass-fed ideal)
- Olive oil, light coating
- Salt and pepper

**For the sauce:**
- 1/2 cup pistachios, rough chopped
- 1 bunch parsley
- 1 bunch Thai basil
- 1-2 cloves garlic, minced or grated
- 1/4 cup or so good olive oil
- 1/4 cup or so red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp crushed Calabrian chilis (from a jar)
- Splash of soy sauce

**For the green beans:**
- 1 bag green beans
- Hoisin to taste
- Sriracha to taste
- Soy sauce to taste
- Garlic powder
- 1 lime wedge

**For the bowl:**
- 1 cup medium-grain rice

### **Mise en Place**

- Wash rice and get it started
- Mince or grate garlic
- Rough chop pistachios by hand
- Chop parsley and Thai basil

### **Instructions**

1. **Start** the rice cooker.
2. **Light** the grill and get it ripping hot. You need ripping heat to get a crust on a thin cut.
3. **Combine** olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, honey, Calabrian chilis, and soy sauce in a bowl. Add pistachios, parsley, and Thai basil. Stir, taste, adjust.
4. **Coat** the steak lightly in olive oil. Season well with salt and pepper. It's a thin cut, so don't go as heavy on the salt as you would a ribeye, but still be generous.
5. **Grill** undisturbed 7-8 minutes, rotating halfway through if you want crosshatch marks.
6. **Flip** and cook to around 130°F. Flank steak is best around medium. I think it's a bit chewy below that.
7. **Pull** the steak and let it rest 5-10 minutes.
8. **While** it rests, microwave the green beans 4-5 minutes. Toss them in a bowl with hoisin, sriracha, soy sauce, garlic powder, and a squeeze of lime. Taste and adjust.
9. **Serve** rice in bowls, green beans on the side. Slice the steak against the grain, cube for Amber, lay it on top, and drench everything in the sauce.

### **Notes**

- We've had some flavorless flank steaks on multiple occasions. The grass-fed steaks from HEB are far better. As of right now, about $20/lb.
- Green beans can be done however you'd like. Microwave is just easy for weeknight and what we like.
- Cook the steak however you'd like and cook whatever cut.
